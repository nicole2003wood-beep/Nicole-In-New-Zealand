Moving In

So, I drove down to my new home. The drive was just over 3 hours, and I had a lovely time. The roads are less scary then I remember, and it was very fun to sing at top volume by myself in the car. Lots of Sabrina Carpenter on the radio atm and she has some very catchy songs. I listened to a podcast, and then once I got in view of the mountains, I took a rest to eat and stretch. I don't remember the scenery being this beautiful before, it really is all encompassing. It did rain the whole way so maybe that made it feel more dramatic.

In the middle of the South Island and right next to the Southern Alps. The views are truly stunning, and I really hope I don't get used to this. The mountains are currently snowcapped, I have been told that in the summer everything turns yellow. Day 2 was very sunny, I saw this gorgeous shaped cloud out the window.
